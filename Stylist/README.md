# Stylist - Personal Outfit Recommendation App

## Overview
Stylist is an IOS fashion assistant that helps users build a digital closet and generate AI-powered outfit recommendations. This app is built entirely in xcode with SwiftUI and SwiftData.
It offers authentication, biometric login, wardrobe management, a style preference, and AI-driven outfit suggestions.

## HOW TO USE THE APP

1. Create Account
   * Open the app and click "Sign Up".
   * Enter your full name, email, password, and confirm your password.
   * Agree to the Terms & Conditions and press "Sign Up".

2. Log In
   * Enter your email and password.
   * Tap "Login"
  
3. Add Clothing Items
   * Tap the "Closet" tab at the bottom.
   * Tap "Add Item"
   * Fill in:
        - name
        - category
        - fit
        - main color
        - accent color
        - brand
        - notes (optional)
   * Press "Save changes" and the new item will appear in your closet.

4. View or Edit Items
   * Open the "Closet" tab.
   * Tap any item in the closet.
   * To remove an item, click the "Remove" and confirm.
   * To edit an item, click the "Edit", update your chosen fields, and press "Save changes".
  
5. Take the Style Preference Picker
   * On the Home screen, tap “Style Quiz.”
   * Answer all questions about your preferred style, fit, colors, and shopping habits, and price range.
   * Submit your preferences so the app can improve recommendations.
6. Generate Outfit Recommendation
   * Tap the “Outfits” tab OR tap “Get Styled” on the Home screen.
   * Select:
      - Occasion
      - Fit
      - Weather
      - Optional notes
   * Press “Submit.”
   * Wait a moment for the AI to generate outfits.
   * Your recommended outfits will appear under “Your Recommendation.”
7. Log Out
   * On the Home screen, scroll to the bottom.
   * Tap “Logout” to return to the sign-in screen.
